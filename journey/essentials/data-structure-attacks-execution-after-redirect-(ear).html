Execution After Redirect (EAR) is an attack where an attacker ignores redirects and retrieves sensitive content intended for authenticated users. A successful EAR exploit can lead to complete compromise of the application. - https://www.owasp.org/index.php/Execution_After_Redirect_(EAR)
