Carvoyant applies best practices to protecting data once it lands in our servers. Additionally, we make it a point to stay abreast of what other parties are doing to protect vehicle data before, and while, it is en-route to Carvoyant.At this point you can Google “Connected Car Security” or “Connected Car Hacking” and come up with a variety of media posts on the topic. Naturally, by the time main stream media is touting the dangers of a topic, those deep in the space have been thinking about that topic for a while. This is most certainly the case when is comes to connected car security.
