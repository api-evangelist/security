As more Internet users become mobile-first, security plays a larger role in the apps and services used in our daily lives. From a business perspective, a company puts its reputation at risk if it is perceived to be lax about verifying user identity.Consequently, ensuring the legitimacy of users has become a critical exercise for businesses, especially community-based ones.Another equally important aspect is keeping the customers’ trust and loyalty.Loss of reputation is expensive because companies need to spend for the recovery of their brand image as well as acquire new customers to replace the ones who left.
