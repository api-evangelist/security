IKANOW’s recap of the major data breaches that took place in March 2015 are listed below.ASML, the world’s largest microchip manufacturer, acknowledged that it has suffered a data breach.The company stated it recently “discovered unauthorized access to a limited portion of its IT systems.” They didn’t state when this breach happened but indicated late last year or early this year.Krebs on Security reported in early March that hotel chain, Mandarin Oriental Hotel Group was the victim of a credit card breach.
