Chief privacy officer Jason Straight shares his insights on why organizations are struggling to stop the breach wave -- and manage the aftermath.Hackers keep on hacking, breaches keep on happening.The cycle continues, as major corporations now routinely get successfully compromised.A key element of the equation now is properly and efficiently responding to an attack as well as managing its aftermath.The same old security missteps--falling for phishing attacks, not locking down sensitive data internally, giving users too much access, for instance--keep recurring.
