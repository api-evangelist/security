The more you know about your customer, the better you can serve them. But what happens when all you know about them is a phone number?Turns out, a good deal.Today we’re happy to introduce you to a new product called Lookup, an API that will retrieve detailed information about a phone number.With Lookup you can present your international numbers in the most local-friendly manner, reduce spam and fraud, and know when you need to use voice instead of messaging.
